Our Story

Built from the Ground Up,
Right Here in the Valley

Chagrin Valley Cornerstone is a family-owned builder with roots in Geauga County and a deep love for Northeast Ohio's communities, landscapes, and architecture.

We build custom homes, additions, and renovations across the Chagrin Valley. What sets us apart is what's never changed: personal ownership of every project, seasoned crews who've worked together for years, and a relentless commitment to materials, methods, and craftsmanship that outlast trends.

Family-owned and operated — the same family name on every contract, every project, every handshake.

Local workforce — we hire and train craftspeople from Chagrin Falls, Solon, Chardon, and across the valley.

Full accountability — one point of contact from groundbreak to final walkthrough, guaranteed.

What We Build

Full-Spectrum Construction Services

From initial survey to final inspection, we manage every phase of the build in-house.

General Contracting

End-to-end project management with licensed general contracting across residential builds. We coordinate subs, schedule inspections, and keep you informed at every milestone.

Custom Home Builds

Architecturally driven custom homes designed around the way you live. From modest farmhouses to sprawling Chagrin Valley estates — built to heirloom standards.

Renovations & Additions

Whole-home renovations and structural additions that seamlessly match your existing architecture. We treat your home with the same care we'd give our own.

Project Management

Owner's representation and independent project management for clients who want seasoned oversight of their own design-build team. We watch the schedule, budget, and quality on your behalf.

Site Development

Grading, clearing, drainage, utility coordination, and permitting. We prepare any lot in the Chagrin Valley — ravines, slopes, and all — for a build that starts on solid ground.
